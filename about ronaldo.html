CRISTIANO RONALDO DOS SANTOS AVEIRO

(Portugal)

"THE RED DEVIL"

Cristiano Ronaldo, a 39-year-old Portuguese forward, is a global football icon. His athletic physique, explosive speed, and exceptional dribbling skills make him a formidable opponent. Ronaldo's attacking prowess is legendary, with a powerful shot, pinpoint accuracy, and an uncanny ability to score from anywhere on the field. Operating primarily as a Striker (ST), he poses a constant threat to opposing defenses. His career has been a testament to dedication and hard work, boasting an impressive tally of goals and numerous accolades, solidifying his place among the greatest footballers of all time.

Ronaldo is renowned for his spectacular goals, including his iconic bicycle kick against Juventus in the 2018 Champions League, a moment that showcased his incredible athleticism and skill.
